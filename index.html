Chenglin Yang

I am currently a CS Ph.D. student at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Alan Yuille.

I received my M.S. in Robotics from Johns Hopkins University. Before that, I obtained my B.E. in Engineering Mechanics from Beijing Jiaotong University, where I am honored to work with Changjiang Distinguished Professor Yue-Sheng Wang . I will never forget the time spent in Mechanics which teaches me to be grateful and concentrate.

Research

I'm interested in computer vision, artificial intelligence and adversarial machine learning. My current research focuses include:

  • high resolution image segmentation
  • knowledge distillation
  • attack and defense in vision system
  • reinforcement learning
